3D Personal Localization (GPS) Software

For my highschool co-op I worked in the VGR (Vision Graphics and Robotics) laboratory at York University. I, and another student, worked with Professor Michael Jenkin to create a demo for personal localization. Using game development software: Unity and programs such as Sketch Up for 3D modelling, we worked to build a very early prototype of a of 3-D real time GPS software that can be used by emergency service vehicles to navigate the streets. I also worked on a Virtual Reality capbility for the software with the Oculus Rift.
